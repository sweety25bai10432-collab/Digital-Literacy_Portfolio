Social Media Do's and Don'ts Checklist


DO'S ✅:

Set your personal accounts to private.
Highlight your projects and skills on LinkedIn.	
Tag organizations/mentors professionally.	
Use a professional profile picture.	
Regularly "audit" your old posts/tagged photos.

DON'TS ❌:

Don't post content that displays illegal acts.
Don't engage in "flaming" or online arguments.
Don't share sensitive personal data (phone/address).
Don't post unverified news or "fake news."
Don't use unprofessional language or slang.
